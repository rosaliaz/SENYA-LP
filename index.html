About us

SENYA is a Filipino Sign Language (FSL) learning app designed to make sign language fun,easy, and accessible for everyone. Using AI-powered gesture recognition, SENYA gives real-time feedback to help users practice and improve their signing. With built-in games and interactive lessons, learning FSL becomes more engaging and enjoyable. Whether you're part of the Deaf community, a student, teacher, or just someone who wants to learn — SENYA is here to support your journey. Together, let’s make communication more inclusive, one sign at a time.
